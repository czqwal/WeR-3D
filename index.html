Why Weight-Bearing?

Traditional supine CT scans often fail to replicate true physiological stress on joints and the spine. This can lead to misdiagnosis, as critical alignment issues and joint space narrowing are only accurately visualized under natural, load-bearing conditions.

Innovation: True 3D Weight-Bearing Imaging

The WeR-3D system enables high-resolution 3D CT imaging while the patient is in a natural standing position. This revolutionary approach provides clinicians with an unparalleled perspective of joint mechanics as they function in daily life.
